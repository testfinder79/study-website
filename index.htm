About us

Study Room Training was created to help Health & Social Care staff build practical skills and confidence. Our trainers have over 25 years’ experience across community, hospital and care-home settings, from Nursery Nurse to Registered Manager. We’re an HCPA Approved Training Provider and our courses are CPD approved.
