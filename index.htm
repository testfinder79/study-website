Study Room Training - Moving and Assisting People Training for Care Homes and Domiciliary Care Companies

Located in the heart of Welwyn Garden City at the WelTech Centre (AL7 2AA), we offer expert manual handling mandatory training tailored specifically for care homes and domiciliary care companies.

Why Choose Our Training?

  • Our training is competency-based, ensuring that staff are fully equipped to handle manual tasks safely and confidently.
  • We offer weekly course reminders directly to your inbox, so you never miss an important training session.
  • Courses are easy to book, with options for booking the whole session or sending staff to our open courses that run regularly.
  • We deliver training in small groups, providing a more personalized learning experience.
  • Our training covers essential mandatory topics such as safe medication administration, pressure ulcer prevention, and moving people safely with dignity.

Key Training Areas:

  • Medication Training: Ensure safe administration of medication to prevent overdoses, underdoses, and missed doses, all of which can have negative impacts on the person being supported.
  • Pressure Ulcers and Friction Prevention: Staff will learn the importance of correct techniques to prevent pressure ulcers and friction injuries.
  • Safe Hoisting and Slings Training: Proper techniques for lifting and assisting people to maintain dignity and prevent injury.
  • Train-the-Trainer 5-Day Course: Develop in-house trainers with a full-day training session for new staff and annual updates for existing trainers.

What to Expect:

  • Evaluations will be sent directly to managers, and staff are expected to pass a certain level of practical competency.
  • We ensure that staff are trained and confident to protect themselves and those they support during manual handling tasks.
